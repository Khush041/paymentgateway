"If we can conquer SPACE we can conquer childhood HUNGER!"
                                                        


HOPE believes in the fact that each and every human being in the world deserves a life with basic need of food and shelter and that is what we are working for. Be their Hope is a Non Governmental Organization which focuses on Malnutrition of children.. So, we strive to provide a better world for these poor kids , struggling for 1 proper meal in a day.
